About Me

UX/UI | Frontend Design | Animation | Startups

Hello! I'm Gino, a Full-Stack Designer (Graphic Design, UX/UI, Frontend Development, HTML/CSS/JS, Rapid Prototyping), startup founder, motion designer and animation director based in Metro Manila, Philippines.

I have years of experience working in a leadership or supervisory role in the tech industry, with expertise in both design and technology roles, and I've worked with hundreds of clients from various industries all over the world.

I'm also the founder of a little animation studio called Stream Engine Studios (http://www.streamenginestudios.com). We make kickass animated explainer videos.

I'm an entrepreneur by profession and a designer at heart. I love networking with other creative, driven and passionate people for possible tie-ups, future projects and additional learning.
